Assignment 1 - The Camera

C) Everyday subject

Select a subject you like and see every day.
Go out with your camera when the light pleases you most.
Shoot the subject from different angles, try to find out the good angle and composition for that.
Select one of your shots as a favorite and return that.
Give that photograph a name. Analyze and explain why you like it the most.

Information about the subject

After asking for a topic of everyday I committed a picture with the sun. But it was only the first time when the sun did not show often. So it turned out that I am a real fan of sauna. I tried to go to the sauna everyday. I am sure that the thing I will miss mostly when I leave Finland are the sauna sessions. The picture shows a smoke sauna, which is located in Muurame. The finnish sauna competition team rated this sauna as one of the best in whole Finland. I visited a lot of different saunas including the oldest public sauna in Tampere and I totally agree with the competition team, this sauna is the best one I have visited so far. This sauna is self-build by the owner and is completely build out of wood.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

I tried to hold the camera as far as possible from the ground when I shot this picture. This angle lets the sauna look big and majestic. Even this picture is made in landscape mode. I cut out a section at the top and also on the right side to fill the frame with the sauna. I used the sharpen tool in Photoshop to make the sauna look sharp and the environment a little blurred. With this effect I tried to create a dept of field. I set up the picture tones to sepia to create an old look and edited a vignette to underline the oldness.

TASK: Find out a subject for your photograph. Take several pictures using a different technique on each separate picture. Once you have exhausted all options, change subject, again shooting several differently composed images. Repeat until you have covered all of the techniques above. Show the pictures to your classmates (done in the beginning of the next lesson) and discuss about the techniques. Return four best shots to your flickr stream and describe the technique you used.

Information about the subject

The stone in the water I see on every bike ride when I go to school. The subject is located near the harbour of Jyväskylä. After we’ve spoken about compositional techniques in the course I recognised that the stone interrupts the pattern of the waves. To be honest, there is nothing special about the stone but it was a nice subject for the composition and light assignment.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

This picture was taken as RAW. So it was possible to play around a lot with the colour canals. I tried to aim the attention on the reflection of the sun with the water. So the pattern of the waves is easy to recognise. Unfortunately I was not able to remove the reflection on the stone without destroying the picture, otherwise the effect would work better in my opinion. I edited out the sheet in the left of the picture but I like the picture more this way. I think it makes the picture look “real” and it is also a pattern interrupting element.

Assignment 4: Creative editing with Lightroom

B) "Shades of October"

Take a picture of a topic "The shades of October". Import into Lightroom and use HSL/Color/B&W panel to create a contrasty Black & White image.
In addition you should make use of area adjustments & graduated filters in Lightroom.

Information about the subject

This is a shoot from the forest next to the freeway near Jyväskylä city. There is „surprisingly“ a lake next to the forest. This forest extends from Roninmäentie to the university and there is a jogging track through it which is used a lot by the locals. The original picture does not look like "Shades of October" so it took a lot of editing work to make it look like a forest in fall time.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

This picture is also taken as RAW. So I had a lot of editing options. First I tried to get rid of all shiny colours. So I reduced all yellow and green colors before I selected the black and white option. After that I cut as much as possible from the top of the picture to fill the frame with the forest. The bottom part I left as far as possible because I wanted to hold the leading lines of the shore. The waves on the water are creating a nice pattern without an interrupting element which makes the non-moving forest look even more “dead”.

Assignment 5: Nature photography

A) Landscape photograph with beautiful light

For time of shooting this, choose either the dusk or dawn. Take a scenic picture, which you compose so that there is a foreground, middle part and background. This means, that your picture should have three layers of image elements. Pay close attention to depth of field: make sure, that it serves the purpose of composition in the image. Also, make sure that the light in this image looks beautiful.

Couple of tips:

Make sure you have enough depth of field by using small enough aperture opening.
Shoot with a tripod! This is crucial because small aperture opening means longer shutter speeds.
Pay attention to focus. You can use hyperfocal focusing on SLR camera.
Watch the light! Make sure you make most of it before it changes!
For HDR purposes most of the time -2 EV, 0 EV & +2 EV are sufficient.

Information about the subject

This is a picture taken from the harbour of Jyväskylä. It was on my way back from dynamo campus and is actually not the one I shot for the assignment. When I past this spot I was in a hurry but recognised the nice sunset and the different layers of the light and decided to take some time and take a few shoots. There are also the required layers in the picture. The first one is the harbour side, the second is the water and the last are the mountains illuminated in a really nice way from behind.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

Unfortunately I did not have a camera with me which is able to take photos in RAW. So I had not that many options to edit the picture. But in my opinion this picture doesn’t need much colour tones editing. I just played with yellow canal to highlight the light above the mountains. In the left top corner there was a bunch of birds I edited out with the stamp tool. And on the shore there were a few people also watching this nice sunset which I let disappear. The last thing I did is to cut out a big peace from the left side because it was interrupting the “silence” of the sunset. From the compositional side there is figure the ground because there is a nice contrast between the sunset and the environment.

Assignment 6: Situational photography

A) Portrait of a stranger

First picture should be a portrait of a person you do not know. Approach a person in the street, a mall, fair or similar place, ask politely if you could take a picture and take the picture. Some tips:
Make sure you have the settings in the camera the way you want them to be before taking the shot. It is not a good idea to start fiddling with things after you have asked for permission.
Be polite. If you are asked for the reason, you can say it is for school assignment. It is actually much easier when you have such an acceptable excuse.
If a person does not want to be photographed, don´t push it. Thank him/her and leave.

Information about the subject

This is a shoot from a very kind stranger in front of Jyväskylän “Old English Pup”. I explained him why I want to take a picture from a stranger and he agreed to take a picture of him. He was the second person I ask. The first one was a women which was unfortunately in a hurry.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

This picture was taken with a Canon EOS 450D so it was possible to take this picture in RAW. This photo is nearly not edited. I just cut out left, right, top and bottom to fill the frame with the person. And edited out a few things in the face. I adjusted the red canal a little up because the jacket and the background are red and I wanted the picture to be a little more shiny. The illumination was perfect because it was a cloudy day but around noon.

Assignment 7: Studio photography

B) Head and shoulders portrait

Shoot head and shoulders portrait using studio lights. Head and shoulders mean that in addition to a persons head, you see his/her shoulders in the picture.

Information about the subject

The model’s name is Niklas and he is also member if the course digital photography. We went together to the photo studio in the main campus. So he was my “model” and I acted for him.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

A head and shoulder portrait as mentioned in the assignment description. We used both studio lights with remote control. The lights stood in an angle of 45 degree (about) in front of him. The camera was on a tripod and we used self-timer mode because we did not have a remote trigger. There was not that much after editing work. I just erased the wrinkles from the background and stamped out a few mother signs. The last thing I did was to cut the picture from all sides to make it look symmetrical.

Assignment 9: Stills and product photography

B) Product photography

Take a product shot of a piece of clothing, ie. shirt, pants, shoes etc. Control the light and the background, if possible use white background.
Think, how to best represent the garment. Should it be on the coat hanger or laying on the white background? Or even on the model?

Information about the subject

The picture shows an advertisement for shoes. The shoes are by the brand dockers and belong to Niklas. This picture was taken near the lake in Myllyjärvi near the public beach on a rainy day.

Reflection about technical & compositional choises

The shoes are the main subject of this picture. So I took the picture from the ground to be on the same height as the shoes. There is also a dept of field, meaning the shoes are sharp and the background around the subject is blurred. This makes an aim onto the shoes. The background is recognisable and this is important because the walls with the graffiti and the stone ground create a nice street look. I edited the string in the bottom of the picture and the price to make sure everyone recognizes that this is an advertisement for the shoes
